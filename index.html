02 — Approach

How I think about the work.

i.

Story first, frame second.

It's not just about looking good. It's about making sure your message actually connects with the right people.

ii.

Details shape the brand.

Lighting, pacing, and sound do the heavy lifting. They create the exact vibe you want your audience to feel.

iii.

Format shapes the story.

What works on a big screen flops on a feed. I shoot and cut specifically for where your audience will watch it.
