02 — Approach

How I think about the work.

i.

Story first, frame second.

Before I plan a shot list, I figure out what the audience actually cares about and what the brand is trying to say.

ii.

Details shape the brand.

Pace, tone, lighting, color — these add up to more than any logo. I treat them as part of the strategy, not as decoration on top of it.

iii.

Format shapes the story.

A film for a website, a clip for TikTok, and a cut for a pitch aren't the same story trimmed three ways. Knowing the format from day one changes everything else.

03 — About

The short version.

I'm a videographer and content developer who thinks about brand and audience as much as light and lens. I came up shooting documentary and brand films — the kind of work that needs to feel honest before it can feel useful — and I'm drawn to the strategy side of brand storytelling: the question of why a piece of content exists.

I'm currently freelancing while finishing my undergraduate degree.
